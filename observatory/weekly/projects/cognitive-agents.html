Overview: What this project is 概述：这个项目是什么

This is the deepest industry research system in the portfolio — L3 cognitive analysis of the semiconductor supply chain.这是整个项目组合中最深度的行业研究系统 — 半导体供应链L3认知分析。

Starting from a single ETF (SMH), the research drilled down through 6 supply chain nodes to 16 individual leaders. Each leader was profiled with 5 source categories, then synthesized into node-level reports with 28-round metacognition traces. The result is a complete top-down chain: Industry → Nodes → Agents → Tickers → Dashboard.从单一ETF (SMH) 出发，研究深入到6个供应链节点、16个个体领袖。每位领袖有5类素材策展，然后综合成节点级报告+28轮元思考。形成完整Top-down链路。

Core insight: Jensen Huang's cognitive model became the template — his "accelerated computing platform" vision explains NVIDIA's moat better than any financial metric. The same cognitive approach, applied to 15 other leaders, reveals the entire semiconductor value chain's decision logic.核心洞察：Jensen的认知模型成为模板——"加速计算平台"愿景比任何财务指标更能解释NVIDIA的护城河。同样的认知方法应用于15位其他领袖，揭示整个半导体价值链的决策逻辑。

Why it matters for trading: Understanding how leaders think → predict what they'll do → identify which tickers benefit. This is the research input that feeds Candidate Workbench.对交易的意义：理解领袖如何思考 → 预测会做什么 → 识别哪些标的受益。这是喂给候选工作台的研究输入。
