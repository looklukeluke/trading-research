TSMC护城河真实且可能比假设更强（亚利桑那良率出色）。新风险是NVIDIA集中度取代了苹果集中度。

KNOW: Gray Zone Warfare Gray zone = coercion below the threshold of armed conflict. Includes: military exercises, airspace incursions, economic pressure, information warfare, cyber attacks. Not "invasion" but still destabilizing. Markets price binary invasion risk but ignore gray zone attrition.
META: NVIDIA as New Dependency Ironic: TSMC diversified AWAY from Apple into AI, but now NVIDIA at 22% is higher than Apple ever was at its peak. The "diversification" created a new concentration on a more cyclical business.

ASML垄断地位不变，但增速可能停滞。如果台积电绕过High-NA，下一个增长引擎就弱化了。
META: The High-NA Paradox ASML spent billions developing High-NA, but its most important customer (TSMC) may skip it. This is a classic innovator's dilemma: the best technology doesn't always win if "good enough" technology is cheaper.
KNOW: Multi-Patterning vs High-NA Multi-patterning: using current 0.33-NA EUV with multiple exposure steps to achieve finer features. Slower and more expensive per wafer, but avoids $360M/tool capital cost. TSMC's math: more multi-patterning steps < buying dozens of High-NA machines.
KNOW: Performance Obligations Performance obligations (€46.5B) = contractually committed future revenue. This is stronger than "backlog" because it includes service contracts. Provides 1.4x current revenue in forward visibility.

HBM超级周期是五个板块中验证最充分的看多逻辑。风险在于择时峰值，而非周期是否存在。

KNOW: Supply Discipline Memory makers learned from the 2022-2023 DRAM crash. Instead of racing to add capacity, Samsung/SK Hynix/Micron are deliberately keeping plants tight. This is why prices can surge 90-100% QoQ -- it's a deliberate market management strategy, not just organic shortage.

07Synthesis: Cracks in the Western Narrative综合发现: 西方叙事的裂缝 + 双向校验完整图景

Summary of Reverse Verification 反向校验总结
Of 5 areas examined, 2 bull cases held up (TSMC, HBM), 2 showed cracks (NVIDIA demand, ASML growth), and 1 showed significant weakness (NVIDIA's ASIC moat). The Western semiconductor narrative is not wrong, but it is more nuanced than consensus pricing suggests.

Specific Judgments That Should Be Adjusted

ADJUST DOWN: NVIDIA Inference Moat Duration
Previous assumption: NVIDIA maintains 80%+ AI compute share through 2028.
Revised view: NVIDIA maintains training dominance (90%+) but inference share could drop to 30-40% by 2028 as Google/AWS/Meta ASICs reach production scale. This matters because inference is the larger and growing market.
ADJUST DOWN: NVIDIA Demand Resilience
Previous assumption: Demand is broad-based and sustainable.
Revised view: 61% from top 4 customers means demand is narrow-based. The "AI capex boom" is really a "4-company capex boom." If any of Microsoft/Meta/Google/Amazon pauses, the impact is severe. GPU utilization rates remain an unverified assumption.
ADJUST: ASML Growth Trajectory
Previous assumption: Steady growth driven by High-NA adoption.
Revised view: Revenue may plateau in 2026 as China normalizes. High-NA adoption delayed and TSMC may bypass it. Growth depends on AI-driven EUV demand offsetting China decline. Monopoly is safe; growth rate is not.
CONFIRMED: TSMC Moat Strength
Previous assumption: Strong moat but geopolitical risk.
Revised view: Moat may be stronger than assumed given Arizona yield success. Geopolitical risk real but timeline is 2027+ for invasion scenarios. New risk: NVIDIA concentration replacing Apple concentration.
CONFIRMED: HBM Supercycle
Previous assumption: Tight supply through 2027.
Revised view: Confirmed and possibly understated. DRAM prices at record QoQ increases. NVIDIA cutting gaming GPUs for VRAM. All three suppliers exercising discipline. Watch for 2028-2029 oversupply as new capacity comes online.
