Phase 0Pre-Research & Framing起点与框架建立 — 轮次 1-6

Round 01 为什么是BTC/区块链？第一性原理推导 Phase 0 · 起点
半导体(SMH)完成L3深度研究后，需要找第二个行业。为什么选BTC/区块链？推导链：黄金成功(宏观储备资产)→半导体验证(Top-down L3管道)→BTC/区块链作为数字黄金的周期特性。 After completing L3 research on semiconductor (SMH), needed a second industry. Why BTC/blockchain? Derivation: Gold's success (macro reserve asset) → Semiconductor validated Top-down L3 pipeline → BTC/blockchain as digital gold with cyclical properties.
Top-down L3 第一性原理 行业选择
Round 02 初始偏差：把BTC当"币"还是"资产"？ Phase 0 · 偏差纠正#1
初始问题：BTC是货币还是资产？这影响整个研究框架定位。纠正：BTC在当前周期已演化为"机构级宏观储备资产"(MicroStrategy+BlackRock ETF)，而非支付货币。稳定币(Tether/USDC)承担实际支付功能。框架应以BTC=数字黄金为核心叙事。 Initial question: Is BTC a "currency" or "asset"? This affects the entire research framework. Correction: BTC has evolved into "institutional-grade macro reserve asset" (MSTR + BlackRock ETF), not payment currency. Stablecoins (Tether/USDC) handle actual payments.
叙事框架 BTC定位 稳定币分工
Round 03 BTC减半周期：4年一轮的"宇宙时钟" Phase 0 · 认知框架
发现BTC减半周期是贯穿整个研究的核心时间框架：2024年4月减半→2025年是"减半后18个月"高光期→ETF资金流放大效应→机构采用加速。这提供了一个可量化的交易信号系统，与半导体行业的"景气周期"框架形成有趣对照。 BTC halving cycle is the core temporal framework: 2024 April halving → 2025 "post-halving 18 months" highlight period → ETF flow amplification → institutional adoption acceleration. Provides quantifiable trading signal system.
减半周期 ETF资金流 时间框架
Round 04 ETH vs SOL vs TRON：L1选品争议与决策 Phase 0 · 方法论#1
L1协议是研究起点，但选哪个？ETH是"最安全但最贵"，SOL是"最快但最中心化"，TRON是"发展中市场隐形巨人"。决策：不选单一L1，而是建立"L1协议"节点覆盖所有主要链，让市场信号决定品种权重。 L1 protocols are the research starting point, but which one? ETH = "safest but most expensive", SOL = "fastest but most centralized", TRON = "invisible giant in developing markets". Decision: Don't pick single L1, create "L1 Protocol" node covering all major chains.
L1选品 多链策略 框架设计
Round 05 从"币"到"价值链"：第一次框架革命 Phase 0 · 转折点#1
关键顿悟：不应按币种(BTC/ETH/SOL)组织研究，而应按价值链位置组织。这个认识来自半导体的"从沙子到iPhone"价值链思维。BTC/区块链的价值链应该是：挖矿→L1协议→中间件→交易所→稳定币→RWA→DeFi→监管。 Key epiphany: Don't organize research by coin (BTC/ETH/SOL), organize by value chain position. This insight came from semiconductor's "sand to iPhone" value chain thinking. Blockchain value chain: Mining → L1 → Middleware → Exchange → Stablecoin → RWA → DeFi → Regulation.
框架革命 价值链思维 半导体迁移
Round 06 初始节点数量：应该是5个还是8个？ Phase 0 · 偏差纠正#2
初稿只有5个节点：挖矿、L1、稳定币、DeFi、监管。问题：过于粗糙，无法区分交易所(CEX vs DEX)和RWA代币化这两个巨大且差异化的子行业。扩展到8个节点，每个节点代表一个具有独特竞争动态和关键玩家的价值层。 First draft had only 5 nodes: Mining, L1, Stablecoin, DeFi, Regulation. Problem: Too coarse, couldn't distinguish exchanges (CEX vs DEX) and RWA tokenization. Expanded to 8 nodes, each representing a value layer with unique competitive dynamics.
节点细化 框架迭代 完整性检查

Phase 1Deep Exploration深度探索 — 轮次 7-18

Round 07 矿机硬件：中国制造 = 下一个"ASML卡脖子"？ Phase 1 · 认知突破
发现矿机硬件95%中国制造：Bitmain 35-45%市占、MicroBT 25%、Canaan 15%。这是整个区块链价值链中最接近半导体"设备商"卡脖子点的位置。如果中国限制矿机出口，全网算力将受重大影响。这个风险在西方分析中几乎不存在——因为他们不关心供应链地缘政治。 Discovery: Mining hardware 95% China-made: Bitmain 35-45%, MicroBT 25%, Canaan 15%. This is the closest to semiconductor "equipment chokepoint" in the entire blockchain value chain. If China restricts exports, global hashrate takes major hit. Almost never mentioned in Western analysis.
供应链地缘 ASML类比 硬件卡脖子
Round 08 英文数据源的TRON偏见：被低估的3.3亿账户 Phase 1 · 偏差纠正#3
英文媒体严重低估TRON。根源：TRON用户主要在东南亚、非洲、南美的"发展中市场"，英文媒体接触不到这些数据。发现：TRON有3.3亿账户、DeFi锁仓量常居前五、USDT最大部署链。这个"隐形巨人"需要单独的代理人研究(Jihan Wu的Bitmain也面临类似偏见)。 English media severely underestimates TRON. Root cause: TRON users mainly in Southeast Asia, Africa, South America — "developing markets" invisible to English media. Discovery: 330M accounts, DeFi TVL often top 5, largest USDT deployment chain.
数据偏见 TRON低估 新兴市场
Round 09 "中间件"节点的存在必要性 Phase 1 · 框架完善
中间件(预言机+数据索引)是L1和应用层之间的"神经中枢"。初稿没有这个节点。发现Chainlink占DeFi预言机70%+份额，LayerZero成为跨链互操作标准，The Graph索引L2数据。没有中间件，整个价值链的"数据流"逻辑就断裂了。 Middleware (oracles + data indexing) is the "neural center" between L1 and application layer. First draft didn't include this node. Discovery: Chainlink controls 70%+ DeFi oracle market, LayerZero became cross-chain interoperability standard, The Graph indexes L2 data.
中间件 Chainlink 价值链完整
Round 10 代理人发现策略：行业影响者≠投资者 Phase 1 · 方法论#2
发现不是所有"行业大佬"都适合做认知代理人。需要区分：行业影响者(能改变叙事)和投资者(能提供Alpha信号)。Michael Saylor是完美代理人(叙事+执行+公开记录丰富)，而很多"币圈大V"只是流量玩家。代理人的选择标准：①改变行业格局 ②有足够公开数据 ③认知独特性。 Discovery: Not all "industry leaders" make good cognitive agents. Need to distinguish: Industry influencers (can change narratives) vs Investors (can provide Alpha signals). Michael Saylor is perfect agent (narrative + execution + rich public record).
代理人标准 认知质量 数据可用性
Round 11 西方叙事缺失：巴西90%是稳定币美元储蓄，不是投机 Phase 1 · 偏差纠正#4
西方叙事把"加密活动=投机"。但巴西数据显示：90%加密活动是稳定币美元储蓄——因为巴西本土货币贬值、高通胀、美元获取限制。这是真实的金融需求，不是投机。这个认知翻转整个"加密市场结构"的理解。 Western narrative equates "crypto activity = speculation". But Brazil data shows: 90% crypto activity is stablecoin USD savings — due to BRL depreciation, high inflation, dollar access restrictions. This is real financial need, not speculation.
市场结构 稳定币真实需求 新兴市场金融
Round 12 e-CNY的特殊性：不是"加密货币"，是CBDC Phase 1 · 认知框架
e-CNY(数字人民币)不能和其他加密资产放在同一框架。它是央行数字货币，有35亿笔交易、$2.4T流通，但完全由国家控制、可追溯、无隐私。放在"稳定币"节点会混淆概念。决策：e-CNY单独讨论，但标注其"合规链"属性影响TRON和Conflux的竞争定位。 e-CNY (digital yuan) cannot be in same framework as other crypto assets. It's a CBDC with 3.5B transactions, $2.4T circulation, but completely state-controlled, traceable, no privacy. Putting it in "stablecoin" node would confuse concepts.
CBDC分类 e-CNY 监管特殊性
Round 13 "交易所"节点分裂：CEX vs DEX的监管逻辑差异 Phase 1 · 转折点#2
发现"交易所"节点内部有巨大差异：Coinbase(合规CEX)、Uniswap(无许可DEX)、BlackRock ETF(传统金融接入)。它们服务于完全不同的用户群体、监管框架和竞争动态。合并讨论会丢失关键细节。拆分为：交易所与托管(CEX+托管+ETF)和DeFi应用层(DEX+借贷)。 Discovery: "Exchange" node has huge internal differences: Coinbase (compliant CEX), Uniswap (permissionless DEX), BlackRock ETF (traditional finance on-ramp). They serve completely different user groups, regulatory frameworks, and competitive dynamics.
节点分裂 CEX vs DEX 监管逻辑
Round 14 RWA代币化：从"小众实验"到"下一个增长极" Phase 1 · 认知升级
RWA代币化是研究过程中最大的认知升级之一。初稿把它当作"DeFi的子集"。发现：BlackRock BUIDL基金$800M+、Ondo 60%代币化股权份额、DekaBank(德国)发行代币化债券、新加坡和香港同时启动代币化沙盒。机构采用正在从"试验"走向"主流量产"。 RWA tokenization is one of the biggest cognitive upgrades during research. First draft treated it as "subset of DeFi". Discovery: BlackRock BUIDL $800M+, Ondo 60% tokenized equity share, DekaBank (Germany) issued tokenized bonds, Singapore and Hong Kong simultaneously launched tokenization sandboxes.
RWA爆发 机构采用 代币化主流
Round 15 数据源偏差：尼日利亚$921亿 vs 韩国$8.44亿/天 Phase 1 · 偏差纠正#5
比较不同市场的加密活动量时，发现数据源质量差异巨大：尼日利亚$921亿交易量来自本地报道，韩国Upbit日均$8.44亿来自上市财报，东南亚市场几乎没有可靠数据。这意味着"市场规模"数字的置信区间极大，不能直接跨市场比较。 Comparing crypto activity across markets reveals huge data quality differences: Nigeria $921B from local reports, Korea Upbit $844M/day from listed financials, Southeast Asia almost no reliable data. This means "market size" numbers have huge confidence intervals.

统计22个代理人的地域构成：美国人/西方12 + 华人/亚洲6 + 欧洲2 + 独立分析师2。这个分布反映了BTC/区块链的全球性，但也暴露了研究偏见：亚洲代理人数据质量普遍2-3/5(语言障碍+公开数据少)，欧洲代表不足(只占9%)。 Geographic composition of 22 agents: 12 US/Western + 6 Chinese/Asian + 2 European + 2 Independent. This reflects blockchain's global nature but also reveals research bias: Asian agents data quality generally 2-3/5, European representation insufficient (only 9%).

Round 17 稳定币：不只是"链上美元"，是金融基础设施 Phase 1 · 认知深化
稳定币的功能远超"出入金工具"：USDT 64%市场份额、$10B年利润(超越多数银行)、在17个以上国家作为实际储蓄工具、TRON链上USDT支持新兴市场汇款。稳定币是BTC/区块链价值链中最接近"杀手级应用"的节点。 Stablecoin functions far beyond "on/off ramp tool": USDT 64% market share, $10B annual profit (exceeding most banks), actual savings tool in 17+ countries, TRON USDT enables emerging market remittances. Stablecoins are the closest to "killer app" in blockchain value chain.
USDT主导 金融基础设施 杀手级应用

Original plan included Aave's Stani Kulechov or Uniswap's Hayden Adams, but ultimately not realized. Hong Kong/Singapore/Dubai attract exchanges/projects with lenient policies; SEC/CFTC create uncertainty moats through enforcement.
