Meta-cognitive Notes

Key Pattern

亚洲数据揭示的最大盲点：西方叙事以美国市场为宇宙中心。USDC vs USDT是最典型案例——西方认为USDC赢了，现实是USDT在发展中市场无可撼动。 Biggest blind spot: Western narratives assume US market = center of universe. USDC vs USDT is most obvious example.

Narrative Bifurcation

存在两个平行的加密叙事宇宙：(1)西方叙事=ETF+DeFi+合规+USDC+Solana；(2)亚洲叙事=稳定币储蓄+TRON+BNB+USDT+直接托管。两者都在增长，但叙事者互相不理解对方。 Two parallel crypto narrative universes exist. Both are growing, but participants don't understand each other.

Data Risk

亚洲数据质量是主要风险。中文来源深度有限，英文来源严重低估亚洲 realities（如TRON 3.3亿账户几乎不出现在英文分析中）。 Asian data quality is the main risk. Chinese sources limited depth; English sources severely underestimate Asian realities.
