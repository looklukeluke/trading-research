Meta-cognitive 元思考

Strength

第二个完整的Top-down L3行业研究。8个价值链节点覆盖从底层（挖矿/协议）到上层（DeFi/RWA/监管）的完整链条。22个代理人包含美国+亚洲+欧洲视角，修正了英文偏见。Second complete L3 deep-dive. 8 nodes from mining to regulation. 22 agents with US+Asia+Europe perspectives, correcting English-language bias.

Chain

半导体验证Top-down管道 → BTC/区块链是第二个测试 → 减半周期+ETF资金流提供可量化的交易信号 → 稳定币+RWA是真正的机构采用驱动力 → 关键品种：BTC/ETH/SOL/COIN/MSTR/MARA。Semiconductor validated the pipeline → Blockchain is the second test → Halving cycle + ETF flows provide quantifiable signals.

Key Insight

矿机硬件95%中国制造 = 类似ASML的卡脖子点。如果中国限制矿机出口，全网算力将受重大影响。这个风险在西方分析中几乎不被提及。Mining hardware 95% China-made = ASML-like chokepoint. If China restricts exports, global hashrate takes a major hit. Almost never mentioned in Western analysis.

Data Transparency

中国全面禁令下的灰色活动难以量化。Tether储备审计争议持续。TRON在发展中市场的真实使用量可能远超报告数字。亚洲代理人数据质量普遍低于美国（2-3/5 vs 4-5/5）。China's grey activity hard to quantify. Tether reserve audit controversy ongoing. TRON's real usage may far exceed reported figures.
