Meta-cognitive 元思考

Strength

第三个完整的Top-down L3行业研究。8个价值链节点覆盖从广告到监管的完整链条。29个代理人包含美国+中国+欧洲+拉美视角，平衡了中西方分析视角。Third complete L3 deep-dive. 8 nodes from advertising to regulation. 29 agents with US+China+EU+LatAm perspectives, balancing Western and Chinese analysis.

Chain

半导体验证Top-down管道 → 区块链完成第二个测试 → 互联网是第三个 → 数字广告+电商+社交是三大流量入口 → 云+支付是核心变现引擎 → 关键品种：GOOGL/META/AMZN/MSFT/KWEB/QQQ。Semiconductor validated pipeline → Blockchain second test → Internet third → Ad+e-commerce+social as three major traffic portals → Cloud+payment as core monetization engines.

Key Insight

中美互联网竞争格局已基本定型。美国主导平台经济(GAFA)，中国主导移动生态(阿里/腾讯/字节)。TikTok是唯一成功渗透美国的中国平台，其地缘政治风险是长期变量。东南亚/拉美是下一个增量战场。US-China internet competition pattern largely solidified. US leads platform economy (GAFA), China leads mobile ecosystem. TikTok is the only Chinese platform that penetrated US market successfully.

Data Transparency

中国公司数据披露质量和频率低于美国同行。中国监管环境(网络安全法/数据安全法)影响全球投资者获取信息的渠道。跨司法管辖区的会计准则差异使中美公司直接对比复杂化。Chinese company data disclosure quality/frequency lower than US peers. Chinese regulatory environment impacts global investors' access to information.
