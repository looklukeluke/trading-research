Deep Dive: The Merge — 技术复杂度与执行风险

The Merge was not just a software update. It required coordinating thousands of node operators worldwide to switch consensus mechanisms simultaneously. The Beacon Chain ran in parallel for ~1.5 years (since Dec 2020) before the merge. Key risks: chain split, validator confusion, MEV reorganization attacks during transition.

合并不仅仅是软件更新。它需要协调全球数千个节点运营者同时切换共识机制。信标链在合并前并行运行约1.5年（自2020年12月起）。关键风险：链分裂、验证者混乱、过渡期间的MEV重组攻击。

Vitalik's role was architectural vision + community coordination, not direct code execution. The actual implementation was split across multiple client teams (Prysm, Lighthouse, Teku, Nimbus, Lodestar). This distributed development model is itself a Vitalik design philosophy: no single point of failure.

Vitalik的角色是架构愿景+社区协调，而非直接代码执行。实际实现分散在多个客户端团队。这种分布式开发模式本身就是Vitalik的设计哲学：没有单点故障。

Deep Dive: Rollup-centric vs Monolithic — 架构分歧的深层逻辑

The 2020 pivot to rollup-centric scaling was Vitalik's most consequential strategic decision. The alternative (execution sharding) would have required fundamental protocol changes, taken 5+ more years, and risked breaking composability. Rollups move execution off-chain while inheriting L1 security.

2020年转向以rollup为中心的扩展是Vitalik最具影响力的战略决策。替代方案（执行分片）需要根本性的协议变更，多花5年以上时间，并有破坏可组合性的风险。Rollup将执行移到链下同时继承L1安全性。

This decision created the L2 ecosystem (Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, zkSync, Starknet) but also fragmented liquidity — a tradeoff Vitalik acknowledges. Solana took the opposite path (monolithic scaling), and the market has rewarded SOL's performance story. Whether modular (ETH) or monolithic (SOL) wins is THE open question.

该决策创建了L2生态（Arbitrum、Optimism、Base、zkSync、Starknet）但也碎片化了流动性——Vitalik承认的权衡。Solana走了相反路径（单片扩展），市场奖励了SOL的性能叙事。模块化（ETH）还是单片（SOL）胜出是悬而未决的核心问题。

03核心判断 Core Judgment

"Ethereum should be the decentralized settlement layer of the internet. L2 rollups are the scaling solution."

"以太坊应该成为互联网的去中心化结算层。L2 rollup是扩展解决方案。"

This is the Ethereum thesis in one sentence. L1 optimizes for decentralization + security (the "settlement layer"); L2s optimize for speed + cost (the "execution layer"). The bet is that long-term, security and decentralization matter more than raw TPS. This mirrors the TCP/IP model: a simple, secure base layer with complexity at the edges. 这是一句话总结的以太坊论文。L1优化去中心化+安全性（"结算层"）；L2优化速度+成本（"执行层"）。赌注是长期来看，安全性和去中心化比原始TPS更重要。这类似TCP/IP模型：简单安全的基础层，复杂性在边缘。

09核心投资问题 Core Investment Question

Can Ethereum's modular architecture (L1 settlement + L2 execution) capture more value than monolithic chains (Solana), given that L2s may commoditize L1 settlement and users don't care about decentralization?

以太坊的模块化架构（L1结算+L2执行）能否比单片链（Solana）捕获更多价值，考虑到L2可能使L1结算商品化，且用户不关心去中心化？

This is the Ethereum bear case articulated precisely: if L2s become the execution layer and users never touch L1, does ETH accrue value? The market is voting "not yet" with the ETH/BTC ratio at 2020 levels. 这是精确阐述的以太坊看空案例：如果L2成为执行层且用户永远不接触L1，ETH能否积累价值？EIP-1559销毁+blob费用是价值积累机制，但够吗？市场以ETH/BTC比率回到2020水平的方式投票"还不够"。

KNOW The Merge (PoW→PoS) ▾

Completed September 15, 2022. Transitioned Ethereum from Proof of Work to Proof of Stake. Reduced energy consumption by ~99.95%. The Beacon Chain (PoS consensus) ran alongside PoW mainnet for 1.5 years before merge. No downtime, no chain split. Largest live infrastructure migration in crypto history.

2022年9月15日完成。将以太坊从工作量证明过渡到权益证明。能耗降低约99.95%。信标链（PoS共识）在合并前与PoW主网并行运行1.5年。零停机、零链分裂。加密史上最大的实时基础设施迁移。

KNOW EIP-1559 Fee Burn ▾

Implemented August 2021 (London upgrade). Splits gas fees into base fee (burned) + priority tip (to validators). During high usage periods, more ETH is burned than issued, making ETH net deflationary. Since The Merge, ~4M ETH has been burned. "Ultrasound money" meme originated from this mechanism.

2021年8月实施（伦敦升级）。将Gas费分为基础费（销毁）+优先小费（给验证者）。高使用期间，销毁的ETH多于发行，使ETH净通缩。合并以来约400万ETH被销毁。"超声波货币" meme源自此机制。

KNOW Rollup-centric Roadmap ▾

Announced October 2020 in Vitalik's post "A rollup-centric ethereum roadmap." Key insight: instead of scaling execution on L1 (sharding), make L1 a data availability layer for L2 rollups. Two types: Optimistic (fraud proofs, 7-day withdrawal) and ZK (validity proofs, instant withdrawal). Current L2 ecosystem: Arbitrum, Optimism, Base (Coinbase), zkSync, Starknet, Scroll.

2020年10月在Vitalik的文章"以rollup为中心的以太坊路线图"中宣布。关键洞察：不在L1上扩展执行（分片），而是使L1成为L2 rollup的数据可用性层。两种类型：Optimistic（欺诈证明，7天提款）和ZK（有效性证明，即时提款）。当前L2生态：Arbitrum、Optimism、Base（Coinbase）、zkSync、Starknet、Scroll。

KNOW ETH/BTC Ratio ▾

ETH/BTC measures Ethereum's relative performance vs Bitcoin. Peak: ~0.085 (June 2017, Dec 2021). Current: ~0.02 (March 2026) — lowest since early 2020. Drivers of decline: Bitcoin ETF narrative dominance, Solana competition, L2 value leakage concerns, lack of ETH-specific catalyst. Historical mean: ~0.05. If mean reversion occurs, that's a 2.5x from current levels.

ETH/BTC衡量以太坊相对比特币的表现。峰值：约0.085（2017年6月、2021年12月）。当前：约0.02（2026年3月）——2020年初以来最低。下跌驱动：比特币ETF叙事主导、Solana竞争、L2价值泄漏担忧、缺乏ETH特定催化剂。历史均值：约0.05。如果均值回归发生，比当前水平高2.5倍。

KNOW The DAO Hack (2016) ▾

June 2016: $60M drained from The DAO (first major smart contract). Vitalik led the community vote to hard fork and reverse the hack. Result: Ethereum (forked, hack reversed) and Ethereum Classic (original chain, hack preserved). The decision established that Ethereum prioritizes pragmatism over absolute immutability — a philosophical stance that defines the chain to this day.

2016年6月：6000万美元从The DAO（首个重大智能合约）被盗。Vitalik主导社区投票硬分叉并逆转黑客。结果：Ethereum（分叉，黑客逆转）和Ethereum Classic（原链，黑客保留）。该决定确立了以太坊优先考虑实用主义而非绝对不可变性——至今定义该链的哲学立场。

KNOW Pectra Upgrade (2025) ▾

Next major Ethereum upgrade combining Prague (execution layer) + Electra (consensus layer). Key EIPs: 7702 (native account abstraction), increased blob count (more L2 data capacity), validator improvements. Expected Q2-Q3 2025. This is the next binary catalyst for ETH price — successful delivery vs delay.

下一个主要以太坊升级，结合Prague（执行层）+Electra（共识层）。关键EIP：7702（原生账户抽象）、增加blob数量（更多L2数据容量）、验证者改进。预计2025年Q2-Q3。这是ETH价格的下一个二元催化剂——成功交付vs延迟。
