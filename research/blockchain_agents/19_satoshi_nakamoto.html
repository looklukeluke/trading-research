01 Overview概要

Historical archive only. Satoshi Nakamoto disappeared from public communication in 2010. This profile exists as a reference document for the foundational design decisions that shape the entire blockchain ecosystem. No active monitoring required.

Satoshi Nakamoto is the pseudonymous creator of Bitcoin, having published the whitepaper in October 2008 and launched the genesis block on January 3, 2009. Satoshi's identity remains unknown. An estimated ~1 million BTC ($90B+ at current prices) in early-mined coins have never been moved, representing the largest single holding in Bitcoin history. Satoshi disappeared from all public forums in December 2010.

03 Action Evidence行动证据

Bitcoin Whitepaper & Implementation
Designed and implemented the complete Bitcoin system: consensus mechanism, transaction model, scripting language, network protocol. The original codebase was functional and secure enough to run for 16+ years with zero critical exploits of the core protocol.
2008-2010 · Protocol design
The Disappearance
Voluntarily left the project and all public communication in December 2010. Never moved the ~1M BTC. This act of stepping away — abandoning both control and enormous wealth — is itself the most remarkable action evidence. It created true decentralization by eliminating the founder's influence.

白皮书核心创新 — 技术解析

1. 双花问题解决: 通过工作量证明(PoW)+最长链规则，首次在无信任第三方的情况下解决数字货币双花问题。

2. 分布式时间戳服务器: 区块链本质是一个去中心化的时间戳系统——每个区块证明某些交易在某个时间点存在。

3. 激励机制设计: 矿工奖励(区块奖励+手续费)保证了"诚实挖矿比攻击更有利"——博弈论最优策略。

4. 减半机制: 每21万块减半，总量2100万枚上限。这是一个预编程的货币政策——人类历史上第一个不可篡改的货币发行规则。

04 Judgment判断

"A purely peer-to-peer version of electronic cash would allow online payments to be sent directly from one party to another without going through a financial institution." "一个纯粹的点对点电子现金系统，可以让在线支付直接从一方发送到另一方，无需通过金融机构。"

The fundamental irony of Bitcoin: it was designed as "electronic cash" but evolved into "digital gold." BTC is rarely used for payments; it is primarily a store of value and speculative asset. Was this a betrayal of Satoshi's vision, or the natural evolution of the design? The 21M supply cap and PoW consensus arguably made "digital gold" inevitable — scarcity plus energy cost equals store of value, not medium of exchange.

META 数字黄金 vs 电子现金

Satoshi的白皮书标题是"A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System"——电子现金。但BTC演变成了"数字黄金"——价值存储。这是背叛还是进化？BTC社区2017年的分裂(BTC vs BCH)本质上就是这个问题的战争。BCH(吴忌寒支持)试图回归"电子现金"，BTC选择了"数字黄金"。市场的选择($1.2T vs $8B)给出了答案。

META 匿名创始人的战略价值

Satoshi的匿名性是BTC的核心资产之一。如果创始人已知：(1)可被政府逮捕/施压 (2)可能因个人言论影响市场 (3)可能动用100万BTC。匿名=不可逮捕+不可施压+无单点故障。这使BTC成为人类历史上第一个真正"无领导者"的货币系统。
