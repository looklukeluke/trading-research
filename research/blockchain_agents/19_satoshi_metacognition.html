§2 Biases 认知偏差

Cognitive biases affecting research and analysis

影响研究和分析的认知偏差

CRITICAL Mythologization Bias 神化偏差

Satoshi is often portrayed as either a genius visionary or a sinister figure. Both extremes distort analysis. Reality: a skilled programmer who solved specific technical problems within 2008 constraints. 中本聪常被描绘成天才远见者或神秘人物。两种极端都扭曲了分析。现实：一位在2008年技术限制下解决特定技术问题的高超程序员。

CRITICAL Identity Attribution Bias 身份归因偏差

Researchers over-attribute features to presumed identities (Hal Finney, Nick Szabo). Each candidate "explains" different aspects, but no single person fully matches the evidence. 研究者过度将特征归因于假定的身份（哈尔·芬尼、尼克·萨博）。每个候选人都能"解释"不同方面，但没有一个人完全匹配所有证据。

WARNING Survivorship Bias 幸存者偏差

We study Bitcoin's success, not the 50+ failed predecessors (b-money, HashCash, etc.). Satoshi built on failures; ignoring this overstates Bitcoin's novelty. 我们研究比特币的成功，而非50多个失败的先驱（b-money、HashCash等）。中本聪建立在失败之上；忽视这一点会夸大比特币的创新性。

WARNING Hindsight Bias 后见之明偏差

"21M cap was obviously brilliant" ignores that supply caps were common in 2008 crypto proposals. The brilliance was combination, not isolation of any single feature. "2100万上限显而易见是天才之举"忽视了供应上限在2008年加密货币提案中很常见。真正厉害的是组合，而非任何单一功能的孤立。

WARNING Authority Fallacy 权威谬误

Bitcoin forum posts treated as canonical truth. Satoshi made errors (e.g., timestamp field usage), walked back claims, and didn't always follow his own stated principles. 比特币论坛帖子被视为权威真理。中本聪犯过错误（例如时间戳字段用法），收回过声明，并不总是遵循自己陈述的原则。

CONFIRMED Anchoring to Whitepaper 白皮书锚定

The 9-page whitepaper is over-indexed. Bitcoin evolved significantly post-whitepaper; citing it as sole authority misses 15+ years of emergent properties. 9页白皮书被过度索引。比特币在白皮书之后显著演进；将其作为唯一权威忽略了15年以上涌现属性。

§3 Corrections 纠正

Cognitive corrections applied to standard analysis frameworks

应用于标准分析框架的认知纠正

Correction 01: Protocol ≠ Person

Separate Bitcoin's technical architecture from Satoshi's biography. The protocol has 15+ years of collective development; attributing all innovation to one person is analytically wrong. 将比特币的技术架构与中本聪的生平分开。协议有15年以上的集体发展；将所有创新归因于一个人是分析上的错误。

Correction 02: Disappearance ≠ Abandonment

Satoshi leaving ≠ Bitcoin failing. The exit was actually optimal for decentralization. A living founder creates centralization risk, regulatory target, and single point of failure. 中本聪离开 ≠ 比特币失败。退出实际上是去中心化的最优选择。活着的创始人创造集中化风险、监管目标，和单点故障。

Correction 03: Electronic Cash → Digital Gold

The whitepaper says "electronic cash" but market chose "digital gold." This isn't betrayal—it's emergent behavior from the 21M cap + PoW design. Satoshi designed the cage; market chose the bird. 白皮书说"电子现金"，但市场选择了"数字黄金"。这不是背叛——这是2100万上限+PoW设计的涌现行为。中本聪设计了笼子；市场选择了鸟。

Correction 04: 1M BTC ≠ Market Manipulation Tool

Despite 15 years of unmoved coins, we cannot assume Satoshi controls them. Keys may be lost, person deceased, or intentional cold storage. Treat as orphaned coins until proven otherwise. 尽管15年来币未移动，我们不能假设中本聪控制着它们。私钥可能丢失、人员可能死亡，或故意冷存储。在证明之前，将其视为孤儿币。

Correction 05: Anonymous = Feature, Not Bug

The unknown identity isn't a research gap to be solved—it's a fundamental design feature. Anonymous founder = no legal person to subpoena, no government to ban, no individual to pressure. 匿名身份不是需要解决的研究空白——这是基本的设计特征。匿名创始人 = 无法传唤无法被引渡、无政府可禁止、无人可施压。

2026-03-22 · Decision 001

Archive vs. Monitor Classification: Historical Archive

Rationale: Satoshi disappeared December 2010, 16 years ago. No active communication since. The "agent" no longer exists in any meaningful operational sense. We maintain this profile as a historical reference document, not an active monitoring target. Unlike Hal Finney (deceased, still monitored for estate implications), or Vitalik Buterin (active), Satoshi's profile serves educational and archival purposes only.

理由：中本聪2010年12月消失，16年前。此后无任何活跃交流。"智能体"不再有任何有意义的运营意义。我们将此档案作为历史参考文档维护，而非活跃监控目标。与哈尔·芬尼（已故，仍因遗产影响被监控）或维塔利克·布特林（活跃）不同，中本聪的档案仅服务于教育和档案目的。

2026-03-22 · Decision 002

1M BTC Alert Threshold: Passive Monitoring

Rationale: We maintain passive on-chain monitoring for movements from known Satoshi-era addresses. No active intervention capability exists (nor should it). If/when those coins move, the market impact will be observable within hours via exchange data. The only actionable intelligence: confirmation that coins moved, not prevention. This aligns with our role as research database, not law enforcement.

理由：我们对已知中本聪时代地址的移动保持被动链上监控。不存在（也不应该存在）主动干预能力。如果/当这些币移动时，市场影响将在几小时内通过交易所数据可观察。唯一可操作的情报：确认币已移动，而非阻止。这符合我们作为研究数据库而非执法的角色。

Identity Speculation Policy: Document, Don't Conclude

Rationale: We list identity candidates (Hal Finney, Nick Szabo, Dorian Prentice, etc.) in the profile with their evidence levels. We explicitly avoid concluding "X is Satoshi." The confidence is too low for any claim, and false conclusions could harm living individuals. We treat identity as an open question with documented evidence, not a mystery to be solved.
