Wu's Bitmain is essentially the ASML of Bitcoin mining — a near-monopoly in specialized hardware manufacturing. The analogy holds: just as ASML sells EUV lithography machines to all chipmakers, Bitmain sells ASICs to all miners. The key difference is transparency — ASML is a public company with audited financials; Bitmain is completely opaque.

Analogy

ASML of Bitcoin Mining

Bitmain is to Bitcoin mining what ASML is to semiconductor fabrication — a near-monopoly supplier of essential, specialized equipment. Both companies profit from the entire industry regardless of which individual customer (miner/fab) wins or loses.

比特大陆之于比特币挖矿，正如ASML之于半导体制造——不可替代的专业设备垄断供应商。无论哪个矿工/晶圆厂赢，设备商都赚钱。

Key difference: ASML is transparent (public, audited). Bitmain is a black box. This opacity risk is the reason for C-tier despite monopoly position.
