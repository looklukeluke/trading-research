§2 Biases 认知偏差 此人及叙事的系统性偏差

1. 行业倡导偏见 Industry Advocacy Bias

Critical 严重 作为矿企CEO，Fred Thiel永远不会建议投资者直接购买BTC而非矿股。他的所有评论都服务于MARA股价和行业叙事。此偏见根植于角色利益。

As mining CEO, Thiel will never advise investors to buy BTC directly over miner stocks. All his comments serve MARA stock and industry narrative.

2. 确认偏见 Confirmation Bias — BTC看涨

Warning 警示 矿企CEO天然做多BTC。Fred Thiel的公开言论高度一致地看涨BTC价格，忽视周期性风险。2022年熊市期间未见相应警示调整。

Mining CEOs are structurally bullish on BTC. Thiel's public statements consistently bullish without cyclical risk warnings. No corresponding adjustments during 2022 bear market.

3. 过度自信 Overconfidence — AI转型执行

Warning 警示 AI/HPC转型叙事极具吸引力，但矿场设施（电力密度、网络连接、可靠性SLA）与超大规模客户需求存在实质差距。CEO的乐观预期可能低估执行难度。

AI/HPC pivot is compelling but mining facilities (power density, network connectivity, reliability SLAs) have substantial gaps vs hyperscaler requirements. CEO optimism may underestimate execution difficulty.

4. 近期偏差 Recency Bias — 2020-2023牛市记忆

Warning 警示 2020-2023年MARA股价涨幅远超BTC，创造了"MARA是BTC杠杆"的叙事。但此表现部分来自BTC历史性牛市，不可外推。

2020-2023 saw MARA stock outperform BTC dramatically, creating "MARA as BTC leverage" narrative. But much of this came from BTC's historic bull run—should not be extrapolated.

5. 生存者偏差 Survivorship Bias

Confirmed 已确认 Fred Thiel引用MARA成功证明矿企策略正确，但忽略了同期倒下的其他矿企（Riot、Hut在特定时期的表现、Core Scientific破产等）。

Thiel cites MARA success to prove mining strategy correct, ignoring other miners that failed (Core Scientific bankruptcy, Riot/Hut performance dips). Success bias in narrative construction.

6. 锚定偏差 Anchoring — 算力即话语权

Confirmed 已确认 "最大上市矿企"成为Fred Thiel的核心叙事锚点。但这一定位在减半后电力成本优势不再时可能成为负担。

"Largest public miner" has become Thiel's core narrative anchor. But this positioning may become a liability when electricity cost advantages erode post-halving.

§3 Corrections 纠正 研究过程中已应用的认知纠正

1. 矿股 vs 直接BTC的beta调整

原始叙事：MARA是BTC杠杆，在牛市中提供超额收益。
纠正：此杠杆是双向的——熊市中同样放大亏损。风险调整后，矿股在完整周期内通常跑输直接持BTC。需用下行波动率调整预期。

Original: MARA is BTC leverage with upside in bull markets. Correction: This leverage is bidirectional—also amplifies losses in bear markets. Risk-adjusted, miners typically underperform direct BTC over full cycles.

2. AI转型叙事需硬指标验证

原始叙事：矿企将成为AI基础设施，估值将重估。
纠正：将叙事与实际签约客户、收入贡献、合同期限解耦。当前AI/HPC转型仍处于"公告"阶段，缺少可验证的收入数据。

Original: Miners will become AI infrastructure, triggering re-rating. Correction: Separate narrative from verifiable contracts, revenue contribution, contract terms. Current AI/HPC pivot still in "announcement" stage, lacking verifiable revenue.

3. 稀释风险的成本核算

原始叙事：HODL策略积累BTC持仓，股东价值最大化。
纠正：HODL策略依赖持续融资（发债、增发），导致股权持续稀释。需计算"BTC收益率vs股权稀释率"的净效应。

Original: HODL strategy accumulates BTC, maximizes shareholder value. Correction: HODL relies on continuous financing (debt, dilution), causing equity dilution. Need to calculate net effect of BTC yield vs equity dilution rate.

4. 电力成本周期性的纳入

原始叙事：低电价地区运营，成本优势显著。
纠正：电力合同期限有限（通常2-5年），到期后续约成本不确定。能源市场再均衡可能侵蚀矿企成本优势。

Original: Operations in low electricity cost regions, significant cost advantage. Correction: Power contracts have limited terms (usually 2-5 years), renewal costs uncertain. Energy market rebalancing could erode miner cost advantages.

5. 竞争格局的动态视角

原始叙事：行业集中度提升，头部矿企受益。
纠正：头部矿企的"受益"前提是需求（BTC价格）维持或上升，且AI转型成功。竞争可能转向能源价格战，蚕食利润率。

Original: Industry consolidation benefits top miners. Correction: "Benefit"前提 is BTC price maintenance/rise AND AI pivot success. Competition may shift to energy price wars, eroding margins.
