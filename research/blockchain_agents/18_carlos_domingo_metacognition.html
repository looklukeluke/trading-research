Authority Bias

BlackRock's endorsement creates strong authority bias. The world's largest asset manager choosing Securitize may lead to overconfidence in the platform's long-term viability. Risk: assuming BlackRock's selection = guarantee of success. Mitigation: examine independent due diligence reports and track on-chain metrics separately.

贝莱德的背书造成强烈的权威偏差。全球最大资产管理公司选择Securitize可能导致对平台长期可行性的过度自信。风险：假设贝莱德的选择=成功保证。对策：分别审查独立尽职调查报告和链上指标。

Survivorship Bias

Focusing on Securitize as the "winner" in RWA infrastructure ignores failed competitors. Many tokenization platforms (Polymath, Swarm, etc.) have struggled or pivoted. The RWA space has high failure rates — observing only successful cases inflates the perceived probability of success.

专注于Securitize作为RWA基础设施的"赢家"忽略了失败的竞争对手。许多代币化平台（Polymath、Swarm等）都举步维艰或转型。RWA领域失败率很高——仅观察成功案例会夸大成功概率。

Recency Bias

The 2024 BUIDL launch and rapid AUM growth create recency bias — recent positive developments may be overweighted relative to long-term fundamentals. Securitize has existed since 2017 with a mixed track record before the BlackRock partnership.

2024年BUIDL推出和资产管理规模快速增长造成近因偏差——近期积极发展可能相对于长期基本面被过度重视。Securitize自2017年成立以来，在与贝莱德合作之前有着参差不齐的记录。

Confirmation Bias

Research began with the hypothesis "Securitize is the leading RWA platform" — this framing may lead to selective attention for supporting evidence while discounting contradictory signals (competition, regulatory risks, technology execution).

研究始于假设"Securitize是领先的RWA平台"——这种框架可能导致选择性关注支持证据，同时忽视矛盾信号（竞争、监管风险、技术执行）。
