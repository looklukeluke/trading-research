Researcher may exhibit tendency to underweight Korean regulatory risks due to unfamiliarity with FSC regulatory frameworks. The assumption that Korean regulatory compliance = safety may not hold given the FSC's historically inconsistent crypto policy.

Recency Bias 近因偏差

Recent 2021 regulatory crackdown may overweight current regulatory risk perception. The Korean crypto market has demonstrated resilience through multiple regulatory cycles since 2017. Past performance may better inform future resilience than recent crisis events.

最近的 2021 年监管打击可能高估当前监管风险感知。自 2017 年以来，韩国加密市场已证明能够抵御多次监管周期。过去的表现在未来韧性方面可能比最近的危机事件更能说明问题。
