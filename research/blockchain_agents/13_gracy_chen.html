🧠 Meta + 📚 Knowledge

META Asian retail model ▾

Bitget represents the "Asian exchange playbook": derivatives-first, social trading, aggressive marketing, emerging market focus. This model serves a different user base than Coinbase/Kraken (compliance-first, spot-focused, Western). Both models coexist because they serve fundamentally different users.

Bitget代表"亚洲交易所打法"：衍生品优先、社交化交易、激进营销、新兴市场聚焦。这种模式服务的用户群与Coinbase/Kraken（合规优先、现货为主、西方市场）完全不同。两种模式共存，因为它们服务的是根本不同的用户。

Knowledge Copy trading innovation ▾

Copy trading democratizes trading skill. Top traders on Bitget have 10K+ followers copying their every trade. This creates a flywheel: good traders attract copiers, copiers generate fees, fees attract more traders. The data from copy trading (what top traders are buying/selling) is itself a valuable market signal.

跟单交易将交易技能民主化。Bitget上的顶级交易员有1万+跟随者复制他们的每笔交易。这创造了飞轮效应：好交易员吸引跟单者，跟单者产生手续费，手续费吸引更多交易员。跟单交易数据（顶级交易员在买卖什么）本身就是有价值的市场信号。
