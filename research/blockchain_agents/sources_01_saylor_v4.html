MSTR股价波动约为BTC的2倍。原因：MSTR以债务融资买入BTC，债务固定而BTC价值波动，因此BTC每波动1%，MSTR股价波动约2%。

MSTR stock volatility is ~2x BTC. Reason: MSTR finances BTC purchases with debt, debt is fixed while BTC value fluctuates, so BTC 1% move causes ~2% MSTR stock move.
